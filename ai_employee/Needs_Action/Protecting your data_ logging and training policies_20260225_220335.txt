OpenRouter never logs your prompts or responses by default.
-----------------------------------------------------------

We only store metadata to keep your service running smoothly:

* Token counts and latency for billing
* Performance metrics for uptime optimization
* Generation activity you can access anytime

You control exactly where your data ( https://openrouter.ai/settings/privacy?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ) goes.

Each provider has their own data retention and training policies, and you can check a straightforward summary here ( https://openrouter.ai/docs/guides/features/privacy-logging?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io#data-retention--logging ). 

By default, your paid requests never route to providers that train on your data. You can customize this further by:

* Setting data policies per request in your API calls
* Configuring account-wide settings in your privacy dashboard ( https://openrouter.ai/settings/privacy?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io )
* Enabling Zero Data Retention (ZDR ( https://openrouter.ai/docs/guides/features/zdr?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io )) for enterprise-grade privacy ( https://openrouter.ai/enterprise?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io )

Want to support model improvements? You may opt into prompt logging for a 1% usage discount ( https://openrouter.ai/settings/privacy?utm_campaign=Welcome+Email+Campaign+%232&utm_content=Logging-email&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ), but that's entirely your choice.
